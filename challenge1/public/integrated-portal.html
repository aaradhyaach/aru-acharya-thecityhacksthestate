Design Principles

A clearer role for every participant

Students describe experience

Students can submit a new issue directly. Selecting an existing work order remains available inside the operator workflow when staff already know the relevant record.

Staff decision support

The current dashboard helps staff review matches, public context, student reports, and priority recommendations. Approval controls, priority updates, work-order creation, and recorded actions are in development.

Verification closes the loop

A work order is not treated as a successful outcome until the people using the building can confirm that conditions improved.

Signals strengthen records

A student report is supporting evidence. It can change urgency, reveal recurrence, fill missing context, or trigger a new review item.
